Design Challenge: Tradovate

Redesign of a Futures Trading Simulator to include crypto.

Tradovate

Final Prototype

Prototype

Problems

Tradovate Futures Pulse is Tradovate's current mobile app offering. They brought some of the styling in from our rebranding exploration, but the UI and UX remain confusing and riddled with heuristic error.

My goal with Tradovate was to eliminate redundancies and mend disjointed user flows. In order to do this, I focused on testing the current product, the current user base and interviewing futures traders.

Research

I surveyed people familiar with Futures Trading, to see what the most important data points were for them.

Personas

Tradovate's user persona is constantly evolving in the global financial market.

Intial Design

Initial designs centered around an easily customizable experience that showcased all of Tradovate's offerings.

Usability Testing

I surveyed people familiar with Futures Trading, to see what the most important data points were for them.

Product

The final product was a clean, dark mode design based on a variety of market offerings and financial data.
